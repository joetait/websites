Go

Go is a two player board game, and according to wikiepedia is one of the oldest board in existence. The rules are very simple, but it is one of the most complex games in existence, as can be seen from another wikipedia link.

The rules are listed very concisely here, and slighlty less concisely, but with useful pictures, here. In my opinion the best place to play online is KGS, though there are servers where you can make a move a day or less. The classic resource for all things go related is Sensei's library. Finally, there is a thriving online go community on Reddit, which has resources page with links to everything else you could possibly want.
