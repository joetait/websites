Abby and JP Advice

Abby and JP

I have two rabbits, Abby and JP (full name Jean-Pierre Serre). Abby is a doe and JP is a buck. Some pictures of them are included below.

They are both from rescue centres, and if you are considering getting a rabbit (or any other pet), I would highly reccommend that you adopt from a rescue centre. There are many places that will go in to detail about why, but the main reasons are that:

  • You know they have a clean bill of health when you get them
  • They are likely to be neutered already, saving you money
  • You can get a much better idea of their personalities if they are a bit older
  • There are already too many animals being bred, and getting one from a breeder just encourages this, where as adopting one helps alleviate this.
